Identifying and evaluating risks that could impact business objectives.

Reviewing the effectiveness of internal controls and recommending improvements.
Compliance Audits:
Ensuring adherence to laws, regulations, and internal policies.
Operational Audits:
Assessing efficiency and effectiveness of operations and recommending enhancements.
IT Audits:
Evaluating information systems, cybersecurity, and data integrity.
Fraud Detection & Prevention:
Identifying potential fraud risks and implementing controls to mitigate them.
